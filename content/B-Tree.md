B-Tree stands for balanced tree. B-Tree refers to the specific data structure and family of data-structures:

- B-Tree (1971)
- B+Tree (1973)
- B\*Tree (1977)
- B-link-Tree (1981)
- B-epsilon-Tree (2003)
- Bw-Tree (2013)
